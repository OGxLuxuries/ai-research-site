Definiti0n:

Artifical intelligence (A.I.) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. These tasks include problem-solving, speech recognition, learning, perception, language translation, and decision-making. Or classically put by John McCarthy: It is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines.
